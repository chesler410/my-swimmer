# my-swimmer — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-07_

my-swimmer is a free, ad-free meet-day companion for swim families. It is built to
collect as little as possible — in fact, **nothing leaves your device.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** my-swimmer has no accounts and no servers that store your data. There is
no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

## What stays on your device

Everything you add lives only in your browser/app storage on the device you use:

- The swimmers you add (names, team, age, gender) and any private notes.
- Meets you import (heat sheets / results / SD3 files) and the times shown.
- Your settings (language, theme, role, team logo).

This data is **not transmitted to us or anyone else.** Clearing the app's storage (or
uninstalling) removes it.

## Network connections

The app only goes online to:

- **Fetch a meet document you choose** (a PDF/results link you paste or tap). For
  links that block in-browser access, the request passes through a lightweight relay
  that simply fetches the file and returns it — it does not store the file or your data.
- **Refresh the public meet directory** ("Find a meet near you") from the project's
  public repository.
- **Load the app itself** and bundled time standards.

Location ("Near me") is used only on-device to sort the meet list by distance; your
location is never sent anywhere.

## Children's privacy

my-swimmer is used by families to track minors' swim events. Because all data is
stored locally and nothing is uploaded, the app does not knowingly collect personal
information from anyone, including children under 13 (COPPA).

## Third parties

We are not affiliated with USA Swimming, Meet Mobile, Hy-Tek, or any meet host. Meet
documents you open come directly from the sites you link to, under their own terms.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change. Material changes
will be noted in the app.

## Contact

Questions? Use the in-app **Send feedback** link.
